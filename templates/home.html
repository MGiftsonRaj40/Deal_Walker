About DealWalker

DealWalker is a dynamic live auction platform that brings together buyers and sellers in a fast-paced, transparent, and interactive environment. Our goal is to revolutionize how online auctions work by offering a seamless, real-time bidding experience that’s accessible to everyone.

Whether you're a seasoned seller looking to maximize your reach or a buyer searching for exclusive deals, DealWalker makes the process easy, exciting, and fair.

Key Features:

  • Real-time Bidding: Participate in live auctions with countdown timers and instant updates.
  • Create or Join Rooms: Start your own auction room or join public/private rooms hosted by others.
  • Smart Notifications: Stay informed about bid changes, timeouts, and auction outcomes.
  • User Roles: Distinct access and features for Admins, Sellers, and Bidders.
  • Mobile-Friendly Interface: Bid or host auctions on the go, anytime.

Our Mission:

To empower individuals and businesses by providing a secure, fast, and fun platform to trade goods through competitive live auctions.


Our Community:

From vintage collectors to everyday deal-seekers, our community is diverse and growing. We believe in fair play, transparency, and user satisfaction.
